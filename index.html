Entity Profile

D.G.P. MELETOUDI P.C. (Doing Business As: D.G.P. MELETOUDI M.I.K.E.) is a leading enterprise in the Greek energy retail sector. Headquartered in Didymoteicho, Thraki, we manage a strategic network of modern gasoline service stations.

Under the leadership of Key Principal George George Meletoudis, we bridge the gap between traditional fuel dealing and modern retail trade. Our facilities are designed to service commercial fleets, daily commuters, and integrate the latest in convenience retail technology.
